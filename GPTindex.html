Education

I completed both my Master's and Bachelor's degrees in Aeronautical Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, graduating cum laude in both programs. My academic career was marked by a strong focus on aerospace systems design, structural mechanics, and control systems, where I developed a solid foundation in advanced aeromechanical engineering principles.

My Master's thesis, titled "Control of a Realistic Multibody CubeSat", involved developing a multibody dynamics model and implementing advanced control systems using MATLAB and AutoLev. I focused on designing algorithms that optimized the CubeSat's stability and maneuverability, applying Kalman filtering and extended PID tuning techniques. This project further solidified my expertise in aerospace control systems.
